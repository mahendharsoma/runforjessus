Join Us as a Volunteer

The Run for Jesus (RFJ) is an evangelical and ecumenical event held with prayer and celebration. As a volunteer, you can offer your time and talents to help share the message of Christ's Crucifixion and Resurrection.

When & Where

This year, we continue the journey, running not just for ourselves, but for those in need, empowering lives and making a difference with every step.

  • Date: April 4th, 2026
  • Locations: 600+ locations across India

Join Us as a Volunteer

Step forward and be part of something greater. Support outreach, logistics, and participant care while helping illuminate communities with the message of Jesus.

Join RFJ Team

Functionalities & Duties Of Volunteers

Volunteers play a crucial role in organizing and executing the RFJ rally. Every act of service reflects Christ's love in action.

Participating in Pre-Planning

Assist in preparation and planning meetings, supporting coordinators and teams before the event day.

Crowd Management

Help guide participants, maintain rally flow, and ensure safe movement during processions and gatherings.

Logistics Support

Support distribution of water, breakfast, banners, flags, placards, and key rally materials at points of need.

Ensuring Safety & Security

Stay vigilant and proactive to prevent incidents and support the leadership team in maintaining order.

Supporting the Local Coordinator

Stand as a reliable support system for local coordinators in all major and minor event responsibilities.
