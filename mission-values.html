OUR VISION, MISSION & VALUES

Run for Jesus (RFJ) is a vibrant movement proclaiming Christ's Crucifixion and Resurrection, fostering unity among Catholics and Protestants. With youth-led outdoor evangelism, media amplification, and engagement with key officials, RFJ spreads hope nationwide. Endorsed by major churches, it continues to expand, inspiring faith and transformation.

Our Vision

RFJ passionately ignites neighbouring communities with the timeless message of Jesus Christ's Crucifixion and Resurrection, inspiring profound spiritual renewal.

Uniting Diverse Christian Brethren

RFJ unites Christians from diverse churches, fostering ecumenism between Catholic and Protestant communities. Experience the power of our shared faith and love as we stand together in unity.

Outdoor Evangelism with Youthful Enthusiasm

Experience a one-of-a-kind event where our youth engage in a sportive display, officially permitted by the police, as we share our faith openly.

Media Amplification on Good Friday and Easter

RFJ secures extensive coverage from print and electronic media, ensuring the rally on Good Friday and Easter reaches a wide audience.

Demonstrating Christian Unity and Strength

Witness the impactful display of unity and strength in your community. Let our collective faith illuminate every corner as we stand together.

Involving Key Officials and Stakeholders

RFJ actively engages police, government officials, and community stakeholders to amplify awareness and participation.

A Platform for Christian Leaders

RFJ provides a stage for diverse Christian leaders to demonstrate leadership skills and strengthen communities.

Celebrating Growth and Impact

From its inception in 2011 with 30 locations, Run for Jesus has grown to 500+ locations, showcasing extraordinary expansion and influence.

Nationwide Rally Expansion

RFJ aims to host a rally in every city, town, mandal, village, and community across the country.

Endorsement by Protestant & Catholic Churches

RFJ is endorsed by Protestant churches and broad Christian fellowships, uniting communities under one banner of witness.
