Be A Coordinator

Are you passionate about organizing and mobilizing for a greater cause? Become part of the Run for Jesus servant leadership team and help bring this vision to life in your city with excellence, unity, and purpose.

When & Where

This year, we continue the journey, running not just for ourselves, but for those in need, empowering lives and making a difference with every step.

  • Date: April 4th, 2026
  • Locations: 600+ locations across India

Lead The Movement In Your City

Take the lead in organizing the Run for Jesus event in your community. Mobilize participants, coordinate logistics, and create lasting impact through faith and service.

Organize RFJ

Functionalities & Duties Of Coordinators

As a Chief Coordinator for the upcoming Run for Jesus event, you guide teams and ensure a successful, meaningful, and ecumenical rally.

RFJ Rally: An Ecumenical Event

Uphold ecumenical values by treating pastors, leaders, volunteers, and participants equally, fostering unity and inclusivity across traditions.

Building The Core Team

Convene pastors, elders, youth leaders, and women leaders to form a committed core team with strong local representation and youth participation.

Fundraising & Financial Accountability

Explore sponsorship opportunities from churches and local partners, and maintain transparent records to build trust and momentum.

Planning The RFJ Rally

Coordinate the route map, flag-off point, closing program, and all logistics including water, transport, worship, and sound systems.

Coordination With Aradana TV

For every new location, consult Aradana TV and obtain approval before proceeding with rally execution.
