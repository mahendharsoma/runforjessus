RUN WITH ONE PURPOSE

Proclaiming Christ's Resurrection

The resurrection of Jesus Christ is the foundation of our faith, symbolizing victory over death and eternal life. Run for Jesus boldly proclaims His crucifixion and resurrection, uniting believers in a shared mission. As we run and walk, we declare the risen Savior and spread His love. More than a physical event, this rally is a powerful statement of faith, a call to action for the Church, and a dynamic platform for outdoor evangelism. Together, let's make His resurrection known and shine His light with every step
